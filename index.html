Additional Information

Transportation

If you are traveling by car, parking is available on-site.

If you are traveling by train, the closest stations are Stansted Mountfitched and Newport (Essex).

If you are planning to order a taxi from the station, here are some contacts of local taxi services:

  1. The Stansted Taxi Company Ltd - 01279 888050
  2. Associated Taxis - 01279 655666
  3. Fem Cab - 01279 414141

Accommodation

We may not have space to host everyone overnight, but you're very welcome to bring a tent and camp in our back garden!

If you'd prefer something with four walls, there are lovely local B&Bs and hotels nearby, here are some options of them:

  1. The Cricketers Army Rickling Green
  2. The Cricketers Clavering
  3. Kings Arms Station Rd Stansted Mountfitched
  4. The Gem Stansted Mountfitched
  5. Premier Inn Saffron Walden
